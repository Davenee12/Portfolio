About Me

Hello, I’m Davenee James, a dedicated Data Manager at Southwestern Adventist University. I specialize in managing, analyzing, and visualizing institutional and workforce data to drive strategic decision-making. With a strong background in Institutional Research, Power BI dashboard development, and data automation, I thrive on transforming complex datasets into meaningful insights. I love to visualize people analytics—diving into workforce trends, engagement, retention, and demographic metrics—to help leaders make data-driven decisions that improve organizational health and employee success. Skilled in Power BI, SQL, DAX, and Excel, I create scalable reporting solutions that bridge data and innovation across departments. My passion lies in turning metrics into stories that empower both student achievement and operational excellence, while also advancing HR’s role as a strategic partner.
