Projects

Student Achievement

This dashboard focuses on documenting and monitoring various measures of student achievement at Southwestern Adventist University. It evaluates how effectively the institution meets its mission and learning outcomes by analyzing key metrics related to academic success. The data presented highlights critical measures such as TExES and NCLEX pass rates, retention rates, counseling exam rates, portfolio assessments for majors and core curriculum, job placement rates, graduation rates, and cohort graduation rates. Additionally, it tracks student feedback, such as seniors' overall experience. The dashboard aims to ensure that acceptable levels of achievement are met or exceeded, allowing the institution to implement changes as necessary to enhance student success and maintain the quality of academic programs.

Trends In Enrollment

This dashboard focuses on showcasing trends in student enrollment data at Southwestern Adventist University, offering a comprehensive and interactive view of the university's student population. It highlights key metrics such as geographic origins, demographic diversity, graduation rates, degrees awarded, and detailed breakdowns by level, admission type, ethnicity, and religious preference. Users can explore data trends through interactive tools, including visualizations on enrollment changes and maps for Fall 2023 and Fall 2024, which display student distribution and demographics. By providing these insights, the dashboard serves as a valuable resource for institutional research, planning, and a deeper understanding of the university's student community.

Fact Book

The Southwestern Adventist University Fact Book serves as a comprehensive resource for key statistics and achievements of the university. It houses essential data spanning from Fall 2020 to Fall 2024, covering information on accreditations, memberships, tuition, fees, student demographics, race and ethnicity, and enrollment figures. This dynamic collection also includes insights into the university's central administration and other notable aspects, providing users with an in-depth understanding of trends and accomplishments over the years. By selecting specific academic years or exploring categories like central administration, enrollment trends, graduation rates, and degrees awarded, users can gain valuable perspectives on the ongoing success and growth of SWAU.
